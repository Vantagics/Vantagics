## AI Generation Guidelines

### Recommended Tools
- **Runway Gen-2/Gen-3**: Complex scenes and people
- **Pika Labs**: Quick animation effects
- **Leonardo.ai**: Static scenes and product displays
- **Stable Video Diffusion**: Transition animations

### Prompt Template
```
[Subject], [Setting], [Lighting], [Colors], [Atmosphere], [Camera Movement], [Aspect Ratio], [Quality Modifiers]
```

### Common Modifiers
- 4K, ultra-detailed, photorealistic
- cinematic lighting, soft shadows
- shallow depth of field
- dynamic motion, slow motion
- futuristic, clean, minimalist

## Audio Requirements

### Music
- **Opening (0:00-0:05)**: Epic tech intro, building anticipation
- **Pain Points (0:05-0:20)**: Tense, slightly stressful, builds to resolution
- **Product Reveal (0:20-0:40)**: Hopeful, inspiring, positive
- **Marketplace (0:40-0:60)**: Upbeat, entrepreneurial, empowering
- **Value Prop (0:60-0:75)**: Warm, reassuring, triumphant
- **Closing (0:75-0:90)**: Grand, confident, memorable
- **End Card (0:90-0:95)**: Soft fade out

### Voiceover
- **Style**: Professional, confident, warm, approachable
- **Pacing**: Slightly faster than normal speech (energetic but not rushed)
- **Accent**: Neutral American/British English (International audience)
- **Tone**: "We understand your pain, and we have the solution"
